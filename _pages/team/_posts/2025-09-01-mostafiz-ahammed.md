Mostafiz Ahammed is a Lecturer in the Department of Internet of Things and Robotics Engineering (IRE) at the University of Frontier Technology, Bangladesh, where he leads this research lab. His research interests include Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence & Neural Networks, Data Science, and Data Mining.

**Experience**

- Lecturer, Dept. of Internet of Things and Robotics Engineering (IRE), University of Frontier Technology, Bangladesh (September 2025 – Present)
- Lecturer, Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering, Chandpur Science and Technology University (November 2023 – September 2025)
- Lecturer, Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering, Notre Dame University Bangladesh (January 2022 – November 2024)
- Proctor, Chandpur Science and Technology University (January 2025 – September 2025)

**Honors & Fellowships**

- National Science and Technology (NST) Fellowship, 2020–2021, Ministry of Science and Technology — awarded on the basis of his Master's thesis (March 2021)

**Selected Research Works**

- *Machine Learning-Based Skin Disease Detection and Classification Using Segmented Images* — M.Sc. thesis, supervised by Prof. Dr. Mohammad Shorif Uddin, Dept. of CSE, Jahangirnagar University. Used Grab-Cut segmentation and GLCM features; achieved 95.68% classification accuracy.
- *Machine Learning Approaches Towards Speech Emotion Recognition* — supervised by Prof. Dr. Muhammad Arifur Rahman, Dept. of Physics, Jahangirnagar University. Achieved an average accuracy of 99% in classifying emotional states.
- *Lessening Coronavirus (COVID-19) Impact using AI Technology: Face Mask Detection* — supervised by Prof. Dr. Mohammad Shorif Uddin. CNN and MobileNetV2-based mask detection trained on the MaskedFace-Net dataset (99.29% training / 98.57% validation accuracy).
- *Brain Tumor Classification based on K-means Segmentation using Machine Learning* — B.Sc. thesis, supervised by Prof. Dr. Mohammad Shorif Uddin. Achieved 92.38% average classification accuracy.
