**A.B.S. Emon** is an Undergraduate Research Assistant at the University of Frontier Technology, Bangladesh, pursuing a B.Sc. in IoT and Robotics Engineering. His research focuses on AI/ML integration for autonomous systems, particularly UAVs, robotics, and edge computing applications.

Emon has published research at IEEE conferences, including "Aerobotics: A Low-Cost IoT-Based Real-Time Telemetry and Risk Prediction System for RC Aircraft" (ICCIT 2025) and "Health Monitor: A Hybrid Edge Cloud IoT Framework for Real Time Health Monitoring" (ICEFronT 2026). His work spans autonomous drone development, ROS2 simulation, n8n AI agent automation, and hardware integration with Arduino, ESP32, and Pixhawk.

His current projects include AI-Edge autonomous Mars rover development with onboard CNN-VLM validation, PX4 drone control using LLM natural language commands in ROS2, and multi-service disaster response drones with wireless power transfer capabilities. Emon has also developed accessible AI solutions through SMS-based platforms to reduce digital divide barriers in rural communities.
