### About the Lab

Welcome to our research lab, led by **Mostafiz Ahammed**, Lecturer in the Department of Internet of Things and Robotics Engineering (IRE) at the University of Frontier Technology, Bangladesh. Our group works at the intersection of Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence & Neural Networks, Data Science, and Data Mining, with applications ranging from medical image analysis to speech emotion recognition and financial market prediction.

The lab builds on a strong foundation of peer-reviewed research, including work on machine-learning-based disease detection, deep learning for healthcare analytics, and interdisciplinary studies applying AI to finance and human-centered signal processing. We are committed to rigorous, reproducible research and to mentoring students who want to build real-world AI systems.

Our director holds an M.Sc. and B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from Jahangirnagar University, and has taught at the University of Frontier Technology, Chandpur Science and Technology University, and Notre Dame University Bangladesh.
